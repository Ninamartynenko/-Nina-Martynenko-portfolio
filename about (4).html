About Me

Welcome! I’m Nina Martynenko, an interior designer and architect based in Ukraine. I have a passion for creating harmonious and functional living spaces that reflect each client's unique style. With over 10 years of experience, I specialize in residential and commercial projects, from initial concept sketches to final implementation.

My work philosophy revolves around simplicity, clean lines, and a neutral palette, allowing architectural details and textures to stand out. When I'm not designing, I enjoy sketching, traveling for inspiration, and exploring contemporary art.
